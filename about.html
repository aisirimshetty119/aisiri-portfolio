About Me

I am Aisiri M Shetty, an actor from Bengaluru with over four years of theatre experience. My journey in acting began on stage through street plays, solo performances and theatre productions, helping me build strong expressive and performance skills.

Alongside theatre, I have worked in television and independent short films including Shree Raghavendra Mahatme, Ghatane, Art of Letting Go, Tuition Love Story and Namma Mannina Kathe.

Apart from acting, I enjoy writing, directing and voice-over performances. I believe every character has a story worth telling, and I strive to portray each role with honesty and emotion.
