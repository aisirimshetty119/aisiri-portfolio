About Me

I am Aisiri M Shetty, an actor from Bengaluru with over four years of theatre experience. My acting journey started through street theatre, stage plays and solo performances, helping me develop strong expression, confidence and stage presence.

I have appeared in the television serial Shree Raghavendra Mahatme and several short films including:

  • Ghatane
  • Art of Letting Go
  • Tuition Love Story
  • Namma Mannina Kathe

Apart from acting, I also enjoy writing, directing and voice-over performances.
